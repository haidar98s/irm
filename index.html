INDONESIA RATTAN MANUFACTURER

Indonesia rattan manufacture is a company engaged in export quality rattan furniture. Our rattan furniture products have been exported to various countries such as Germany, France, Holland, Austria, and others. Indonesia is the largest rattan producing country in the world, it is estimated that 80% of rattan raw materials worldwide are produced by Indonesia.

We see a need for international quality rattan woven. We produce rattan furniture with export quality and have experience in fulfilling the needs of rattan furniture made from natural rattan as well as natural / synthetic rattan.
